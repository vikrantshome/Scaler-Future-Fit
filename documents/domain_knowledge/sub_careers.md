# Engineering Sub-Careers Guide

This document details the specific career paths available within each major engineering branch, including role descriptions and top recruiters in India.

## 1. Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
*   **Software Developer:** Coding & debugging software. *Recruiters:* Google, Microsoft, TCS.
*   **Data Scientist:** Analyze data for insights. *Recruiters:* Deloitte, Amazon, Fractal.
*   **Cloud & DevOps:** Manage cloud infra & pipelines. *Recruiters:* AWS, IBM, Accenture.
*   **Cybersecurity:** Protect systems from threats. *Recruiters:* EY, Wipro, Palo Alto.

## 2. AI & Data Science
*   **ML Engineer:** Build predictive AI models. *Recruiters:* OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Uber.
*   **Data Analyst:** Visualize and interpret data. *Recruiters:* Mu Sigma, ZS Associates.
*   **AI Researcher:** Invent new AI algorithms. *Recruiters:* Microsoft Research, IBM Research.

## 3. Information Technology (IT)
*   **Full Stack Developer:** Web & App development. *Recruiters:* Tech Startups, Infosys.
*   **Network Admin:** Manage IT networks & security. *Recruiters:* Cisco, Wipro.
*   **Business Analyst:** Bridge business & tech needs. *Recruiters:* McKinsey, Accenture.
*   **QA Engineer:** Test software for bugs. *Recruiters:* Capgemini, Cognizant.

## 4. Electronics & Communication (ECE)
*   **Embedded Systems:** Code for microcontrollers. *Recruiters:* Bosch, Continental, Qualcomm.
*   **VLSI Engineer:** Design chips & circuits. *Recruiters:* Intel, NVIDIA, AMD.
*   **Telecom Engineer:** Manage wireless networks. *Recruiters:* Jio, Airtel, Ericsson.
*   **IoT Specialist:** Connect devices to internet. *Recruiters:* Samsung, Honeywell.

## 5. Mechanical Engineering
*   **Design Engineer:** CAD/CAE product design. *Recruiters:* Tata Motors, Mahindra.
*   **Production Engineer:** Manage manufacturing lines. *Recruiters:* Maruti Suzuki, L&T.
*   **Automotive Engineer:** Design vehicle systems. *Recruiters:* Mercedes-Benz, Toyota.
*   **Robotics Engineer:** Build automated machines. *Recruiters:* GreyOrange, Fanuc.

## 6. Electrical Engineering
*   **Power Systems Eng:** Grid & substation design. *Recruiters:* PowerGrid, NTPC, Tata Power.
*   **Control Engineer:** Automation & PLC systems. *Recruiters:* Siemens, ABB, Schneider.
*   **Renewable Energy:** Solar & Wind projects. *Recruiters:* Adani Green, ReNew Power.

## 7. Civil Engineering
*   **Structural Engineer:** Design safe buildings. *Recruiters:* L&T Construction, DLF.
*   **Project Manager:** Oversee site execution. *Recruiters:* Shapoorji Pallonji, GMR.
*   **Transport Engineer:** Plan roads & traffic. *Recruiters:* NHAI, RITES.

## 8. Chemical Engineering
*   **Process Engineer:** Design plant processes. *Recruiters:* Reliance, Indian Oil.
*   **R&D Scientist:** Develop new materials. *Recruiters:* Asian Paints, Dr. Reddy's.
*   **Safety Engineer:** Ensure plant safety. *Recruiters:* DuPont, Shell.

## 9. Aerospace Engineering
*   **Avionics Engineer:** Aircraft electronics. *Recruiters:* HAL, Boeing, Airbus.
*   **Aerodynamics Eng:** Airflow & propulsion. *Recruiters:* ISRO, DRDO, GE Aviation.
*   **Maintenance Eng:** Aircraft servicing. *Recruiters:* IndiGo, Air India.

## 10. Biotechnology
*   **Bioprocess Eng:** Manage bio-manufacturing. *Recruiters:* Biocon, Serum Institute.
*   **Clinical Researcher:** Drug trials & data. *Recruiters:* Pfizer, Novartis.
*   **Bioinformatics:** Biological data analysis. *Recruiters:* Strand Life Sciences.

## 11. Industrial & Production Engineering
*   **Operations Manager:** Oversee factory output. *Recruiters:* ITC, HUL, Nestle.
*   **Supply Chain Analyst:** Logistics & distribution. *Recruiters:* Flipkart, Amazon.
*   **Quality Engineer:** Six Sigma & reliability. *Recruiters:* Toyota, General Electric.

## 12. Algorithmic Trading & FinTech
*   **Quant Researcher:** Math models for markets. *Recruiters:* Tower Research, Jane Street.
*   **HFT Developer:** Low-latency trading code. *Recruiters:* Optiver, Graviton.
*   **FinTech Engineer:** Payment & banking apps. *Recruiters:* Razorpay, PhonePe, Zerodha.
