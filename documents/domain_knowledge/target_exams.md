# Target Exams Guide for Engineering Aspirants

This document outlines the key competitive exams relevant to various engineering branches in India, categorized by their scope (National, State, Private, New-age).

## 1. Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
*   **National:**
    *   **JEE Main:** Primary gateway to NITs, IIITs, and other centrally funded institutes.
    *   **JEE Advanced:** The sole entrance for the prestigious IITs.
*   **Private (National Level):**
    *   **BITSAT:** For BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses. Known for high packages.
    *   **VITEEE:** Admittance to Vellore Institute of Technology.
    *   **SRMJEEE:** For SRM Institute of Science and Technology campuses.
*   **State Level (Top):**
    *   **MHT-CET:** Maharashtra state colleges (COEP, VJTI).
    *   **KCET:** Karnataka state colleges (RVCE, BMS).
*   **New-Age / Outcome-Driven:**
    *   **Scaler NSET:** Admission test for Scaler School of Technology (Specialized CS program).
    *   **IIIT-H UGEE:** For dual-degree research programs at IIIT Hyderabad.

## 2. Electronics & Communication (ECE)
*   **National:** JEE Main, JEE Advanced.
*   **Private:** BITSAT, VITEEE, SRMJEEE, Manipal MET.
*   **State:** MHT-CET, KCET, WBJEE.
*   **New-Age:** IIIT-H UGEE (Research in ECE/Signal Processing).

## 3. Mechanical Engineering
*   **National:** JEE Main, JEE Advanced.
*   **Private:** BITSAT (Strong Mechanical dept), VITEEE.
*   **State:** MHT-CET (Pune is a manufacturing hub), COMEDK (Karnataka private colleges).

## 4. Electrical Engineering
*   **National:** JEE Main, JEE Advanced.
*   **Private:** BITSAT (Electrical & Electronics), VITEEE.
*   **State:** WBJEE, MHT-CET.

## 5. Civil Engineering
*   **National:** JEE Main, JEE Advanced.
*   **Private:** BITSAT, SRMJEEE.
*   **State:** MHT-CET, AP EAPCET.

## 6. Chemical Engineering
*   **National:** JEE Main, JEE Advanced.
*   **Private:** BITSAT (Process Engineering).
*   **State:** WBJEE (Jadavpur University), MHT-CET (ICT Mumbai).

## 7. Aerospace Engineering
*   **National:** JEE Advanced (IIT Bombay, IIT Kharagpur), JEE Main (PEC, IIST).
*   **Private:** BITSAT, Manipal MET.
*   **New-Age:** IIIT-H UGEE (Avionics research overlap).

## 8. Biotechnology
*   **National:** JEE Main.
*   **Private:** BITSAT (M.Sc. Bio), VITEEE.
*   **New-Age:** IIIT-H UGEE (Computational Biology).

## 9. Information Technology (IT)
*   **National:** JEE Main.
*   **Private:** VITEEE, SRMJEEE.
*   **New-Age:** Scaler NSET (Direct pathway to Software Engineering careers).

## 10. Algorithmic Trading & FinTech
*   **National:** JEE Advanced (IIT Mathematics & Computing / Statistics).
*   **New-Age:** Scaler NSET (Curriculum includes Algorithmic Trading specialization).
*   **Private:** BITSAT (M.Sc. Economics + B.E. CS dual degree).
*   **Other:** ISI Admission Test (Indian Statistical Institute).
